About

This web-based platform for online resources of academic journals for
Korean history, including Hanguksa Yeongu Hwibo (Bulletin of Korean Historical Research) available in Korean is constructed with additional online training materials and guidelines of bibliographic analysis and metadata research for librarians and scholars in Digital Humanities and Social Sciences. The tutorials and guidelines  specify what resources can be used in what format and how and when they can be used to serve their research.
